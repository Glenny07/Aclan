Who we are

Aclan & Co. CPAs started in 1987 under the name of Aclan Accounting & Auditing Office. For the past 25 years, the firm existed as a single proprietorship until it was finally transformed into a partnership on May 2012.

What we do

Aclan & Co. CPAs provides a variety of business services to companies in various industries. The firm focuses on auditing, accounting and tax planning services as well as business advisory and consultancy.

Why choose us

Our staff comprise of a group of senior and junior associates and the firm also collaborate with a pool of specialized consultants in various fields of experience to perform consulting engagements and support operation.

Why join us

Our partners advocate a continuous staff professional development through training programs to ensure and maintain highly qualified and competent staff that support the employee's career development through lifetime employment.

As preferred provider of our clients, we shall deliver our services with:

Credibility and Efficiency, accessibility and responsiveness, and Reliability and Dependability. We know that our progress and success is dependent on our commitment to excellence together with the professional services we provide to our clients.

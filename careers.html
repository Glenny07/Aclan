Office Assistant - Qualifications
Qualifications
  • Candidate must possess at least a Bachelor’s/College degree in any business field.
  • Must be knowledgeable with computers.
  • Fresh graduates and entry-level applicants are preferred and encouraged to apply.
Responsibilities:
  • Deliver messages, transport packages, mail and other items from one place of business to another.
  • Determine destinations by referring to addresses on packages and documents.
  • Ensure that packages are ready for delivery by packing them in a safe manner.
  • Ensure that packages or messages are delivered in a time efficient manner.
  • Sort deliverable items in accordance to delivery route in order to ensure maximum efficiency.
  • Maintain records of daily visits in the log book.
  • Understand privacy rules and not tamper with package or document.
  • Plan efficient routes prior to setting out on a delivery mission.
  • Protect organization’s values by keeping information confidential.
  • Maintain financial security by adhering to internal controls.
Other Details:
Proficiencies:
  • Microsoft Office Proficient (Mandatory)

Full-Time
Working Hours:
Regular Hours (Monday-Friday)
